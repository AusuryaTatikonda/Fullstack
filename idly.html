IDLI

Ingredients:

  • idli rice (parboiled rice)
  • urad dal (split black gram)
  • fenugreek seeds (methi seeds)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water

Equipment:

  1. Idli steamer or pressure cooker with idli plates
  2. Idli molds (idli stand)

Recipe making

1.Wash the idli rice and urad dal separately a couple of times under running water until the water runs clear. Soak the idli rice and fenugreek seeds together in a bowl with enough water for about 4-6 hours. Soak the urad dal in another bowl with enough water for about 4-6 hours.
2.Drain the water from the soaked urad dal and grind it in a wet grinder or blender. Add water gradually and grind until it becomes smooth and fluffy. The consistency should be like whipped cream. Drain the water from the soaked rice and grind it to a smooth paste. You may need to add water gradually to achieve a smooth consistency. Mix the ground urad dal and rice batter together in a large mixing bowl. Add salt to taste and mix well. The batter should have a thick pouring consistency.
3.Cover the bowl with a clean cloth or lid and allow it to ferment for 8-12 hours or overnight. Keep it in a warm place; a turned-off oven with the light on is a good option. After fermentation, the batter will have risen and become slightly fluffy.
4.Grease the idli molds with a little oil or ghee to prevent sticking. Pour a ladleful of the fermented idli batter into each mold, filling them about 3/4 full. Place the idli stand or molds into a steamer or pressure cooker with some water at the bottom. Ensure that the molds do not touch the water. Steam the idlis on medium heat for about 10-15 minutes. You can insert a toothpick into an idli to check if it's done; if the toothpick comes out clean, the idlis are ready.
5.Carefully remove the idli molds from the steamer or pressure cooker and let them cool for a minute. Use a spoon or a butter knife to gently scoop the idlis out of the molds. They should come out easily.

Serve and enjoy hot idlis with coconut chutney, sambar, or your choice of side dish...
